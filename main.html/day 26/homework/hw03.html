My Favorite Olympic Champion

Usain Bolt

Usain Bolt is a Jamaican sprinter who is widely considered the greatest sprinter of all time. He holds the world records for the 100 meters, 200 meters, and 4 × 100 meters relay. Bolt has won eight Olympic gold medals and is known for his incredible speed and charismatic personality.
